Where the stars come out to play

Just off Highway 60 in Magdalena — gateway to the Plains of San Agustin — lies Magdalena Motor Inn. Set beneath some of North America's darkest skies, we provide a comfortable basecamp for astronomers, hunters, VLA visitors, and anyone seeking the quiet magic of the high desert.

Dark Sky Paradise

Magdalena sits in one of the darkest corners of New Mexico. With virtually no light pollution, our skies reveal the Milky Way in stunning clarity. Whether you're here for astrophotography or simply to marvel at the cosmos, you've found the right place.

Dark Sky Image

Minutes from the VLA

The Very Large Array — one of the world's premier astronomical radio observatories — is just down the road. Walk among the iconic 27 dish antennas and explore the visitor center during your stay.

Hunter & Angler Friendly

Freezer space for your harvest. Early check-ins. Local knowledge of the best spots. We understand what you need because we're part of this landscape too.
